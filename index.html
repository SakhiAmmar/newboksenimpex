Have a question? Check out the FAQ

sportswear and leather product manufacturer like Boksen Impex:

What products do you manufacture?

We specialize in custom sportswear (boxing, MMA, gym wear, football kits, tracksuits) and leather goods (boxing gloves, driving gloves, gym accessories, etc.).

Can I add my own logo and design?

Yes! We offer full customization including logo printing, embroidery, labeling, and packaging. Just share your design or concept and we’ll create a digital mockup.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ depends on the product, but we offer low MOQs to support startups and small businesses. Please contact us for specific details.

Do you offer samples before bulk production?

Yes, we can provide samples so you can review quality, sizing, and customization before placing a full order.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 2–4 weeks depending on the product and quantity. Express production and shipping are also available on request.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide via trusted couriers. We’ll provide a tracking number once your order is dispatched, and we assist with customs if needed.
